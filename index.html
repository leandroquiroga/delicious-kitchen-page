Classic Cheescake Recipe

Look no further fir creany and ultra smooth classic cheescake recipe! Paired with a buttery graham cracjer crustm no one can deny its simple decadence. For the best result, bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham crackers)

5 Tablespoons (70g) unsaled butter melted

1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

Cheescake

four 8-once blocks (904g) full-fat graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham crackers)

unsaled butter melted

granulated sugar
